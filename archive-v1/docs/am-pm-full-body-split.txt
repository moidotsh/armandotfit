2-A-DAY FULL BODY SPLIT (4 days/week)
Each day = 2 workouts (AM + PM) of 4 exercises

DAY 1 – Chest Press / Triceps / Side Delts
🟦 AM
* Chest – Incline Barbell Press
* UpperLeg – Leg Press
* Arms – Overhead Cable Tricep Extension
* Shoulders – Egyptian Cable Lateral Raise

🟨 PM
* LowerLeg – Tibia Raise Machine
* Back – Lower Back Extension
* Abs – Captain's Chair Leg Raise
* Arms – Cable Tricep Kickback

DAY 2 – Chest Fly / Biceps / Rear Delts
🟦 AM
* Back – Seated Cable Row
* Arms – Incline Dumbbell Curl
* Chest – Machine Fly
* UpperLeg – Seated Leg Curl

🟨 PM
* Shoulders – Cable Reverse Flyes
* LowerLeg – Standing Calf Raise
* Back/Shoulders – Rope Grip Face Pull
* Abs – Machine Ab Crunch

DAY 3 – Chest Press / Triceps / Side Delts
🟦 AM
* Chest – Incline Dumbbell Press
* Arms – Machine Tricep Dip
* Shoulders – Chest-Supported Dumbbell Lateral Raise
* UpperLeg – Bulgarian Split Squat

🟨 PM
* LowerLeg – Tibia Raise Machine
* Back – Dumbbell Pullover
* Abs – Captain's Chair Leg Raise
* UpperLeg (Accessory) – Hip Adduction Machine

DAY 4 – Chest Fly / Biceps / Rear Delts
🟦 AM
* Back – Reverse Grip Lat Pulldown
* UpperLeg – Leg Extension
* Chest – Incline Dumbbell Fly
* Arms – Reverse Curl + Hammer Curl Superset

🟨 PM
* Shoulders – Rope Face Pulls
* LowerLeg – Leg Press Calf Raise
* Abs – Machine Ab Crunch
* Back – Chest-Supported Lever Row
