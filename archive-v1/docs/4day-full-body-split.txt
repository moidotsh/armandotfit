4-DAY FULL BODY SPLIT

DAY 1
---
Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Bent Over Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Pull-ups or Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Leg Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

DAY 2
---
Deadlifts: 3 sets of 5-8 reps
Incline Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Seated Cable Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Leg Extensions: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

DAY 3
---
Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
T-Bar Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Cable Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

DAY 4
---
Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Dips: 3 sets to failure
Chin-ups: 3 sets to failure
Pec Deck or Flyes: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Tricep Dips (machine or bench): 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Barbell Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

NOTES
- Rest 90-120 seconds between sets
- Use weight that challenges you but maintains proper form
- Progressive overload: increase weight or reps each week
- Take rest days between workout days as needed
- Warm up with 5-10 minutes of light cardio
- Cool down and stretch after each workout
